Blueberry Drizzle Pancakes

Description

This is the perfect blueberry pancake recipe! The blueberry taste really pops!

Of course, to make this recipe complete, we highly recommend adding the quick warm blueberry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten until foamy
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1-1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1-1/2 cups fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. In large bowl, blend together the flour, sugar, baking pwder, cinnamon, blueberries, and salt.
  2. In medium bowl, beat eggs until foamy. Beat in milk and butter.
  3. Combine the two mixtures, stirring until the ingredients are combined but the batter is still slightly lumpy.
  4. Brush skillet with oil. Drop a heaping serving in prepared skillet and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Turn pancake and brown the other side.
  5. Serve with Quick Warm Blueberry Sauce.
