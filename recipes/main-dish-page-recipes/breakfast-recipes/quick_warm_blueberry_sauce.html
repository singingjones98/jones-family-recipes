Quick Warm Blueberry Sauce

Description

A rich, delectable topping for breakfast!

Made specifically for Blueberry Drizzle Pancakes, but would be delicious for most pancakes or waffles.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon corn starch
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 4 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup orange juice

Steps

  1. Combine blueberries, sugar and 1/4 cup orange juice in sauce pan. Warm mixture while stirring occasionally.
  2. Blend cornstarch with remaining orange juice. Remove any lumps. Bring to a boil; add to sugar mixture.
  3. Cook the sauce at a low boil for 1 minute, stirring occasionally.
  4. Remove the pan from heat and stir in lemon juice; will thicken as it cools.
