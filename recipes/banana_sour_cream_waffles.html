Banana Sour Cream Waffles

Description

This is a really yummy recipe! It has a rich taste with lots of flavor

You can add maple syrup or other toppings, but it even tastes delicious all by itself!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, separated
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 banana, mashed

Steps

  1. Sift together dry ingredients. Set Aside.
  2. Mix together beaten egg yolk, sour cream, milk, butter, and banana. Add to flour mixture, mixing well.
  3. Fold in whipped egg white.
  4. Pour into oiled waffle iron or oiled pan. Cook each side until brown.
